Process

How EvidenceChain Works

Our blockchain technology ensures a secure, transparent, and verifiable process for managing digital evidence.

1

Capture & Upload

Collect digital evidence through our secure mobile app or web interface. All files are automatically timestamped and encrypted during upload.

2

Blockchain Verification

Each piece of evidence is hashed and recorded on the blockchain, creating an immutable record that proves authenticity and prevents tampering.

3

Secure Storage

Evidence is encrypted and stored in our secure cloud infrastructure with redundant backups, ensuring it's always available when needed.

4

Chain of Custody

Every access, view, or modification is automatically logged on the blockchain, creating a verifiable chain of custody record.

5

Court-Ready Export

Generate comprehensive reports with blockchain verification certificates that prove the evidence hasn't been tampered with.
